Freehand Art

Freehand painting in nail art is a technique where intricate designs are created directly on the nail surface using small brushes and specialized nail art paints or acrylics. It offers limitless possibilities for creativity and allows nail artists to showcase their skills and artistic flair.

Brushes used in freehand art are-

  • Detailing brush
  • Dotting tool

    • Stamp Art

    A pre-designed pattern is stamped onto the nails using a stamping plate, a special nail polish, and a stamper. It is a quick way to achieve intricate designs.

    Decals And Sticker Art

    Pre-made designs, decals, or stickers are applied onto the nails. They come in various shapes, colors, and patterns, making it easy to achieve intricate designs without much effort.

    3D Art

    Small beads, rhinestones, studs, glitter, or other decorative materials are applied to the nails to add texture and dimension. These embellishments can be glued onto the nails or embedded in gel or acrylic extensions.

    Marble Art

    A unique technique where drops of nail polish are added to water, creating a marbled effect. Nails are then dipped into the water, transferring the design onto the nail surface.
